We The Jury: Brandeis University

We look to encompass all aspects of Athletics by focusing on every sport to reward students for school spirit and involvement. This increase in student attendance will provide an advantage on the playing field for all student-athletes as well as enhance the overall experience for the fans while raising the level of pride for Brandeis on and off campus. In addition, students can earn bonus points throughout the year. These opportunities will come from the Brandeis University Athletics Facebook page and follow us @BrandeisJudges on Twitter and Instagram. By accumulating points, participants can earn FREE rewards as they hit each point plateau.

We The Jury has been designed to reward students based on their attendance at athletic events.

1 point Mens & Womens Basketball 2 points Baseball, Softball, Men’s and Women’s Soccer, and Bus Trips 3 points Volleyball, Track and Field, Men’s and Women’s Tennis, Men’s and Women’s Fencing, and Men’s and Women’s Swimming and Diving 4 points #JuryDuty Events
